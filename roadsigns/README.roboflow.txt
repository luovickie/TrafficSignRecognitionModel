
Traffic and Road Signs - v1 2022-03-28 2:27pm
==============================

This dataset was exported via roboflow.ai on April 1, 2022 at 10:04 AM GMT

It includes 10000 images.
Traffic-signs are annotated in Multi-Class Classification format.

The following pre-processing was applied to each image:
* Auto-orientation of pixel data (with EXIF-orientation stripping)
* Resize to 416x416 (Stretch)

No image augmentation techniques were applied.
